Our Mission Statement

The Medford Animal Rescueâ€™s mission is to rescue the lost, abandoned, and mistreated animals and provide for their relief and safety until they are reunited with a loving owner or placed in a new home. We are committed to the health and wellness for our animals, which means no pet will ever be euthanized because it has run out of time to find a home. We are committed to ending the overpopulation of pets that results in millions of companion pets being euthanized at shelters in the U.S. every year, therefore all cats, rabbits, and dogs adopted from us are spayed and neutered prior to adoption. We would appreciate your contributions and your willingness to join us in our journey!

Ways to give

Volunteer with us!

  • Dog care: spend time directly with our dogs and help them form healthy bonds via positive reinforcement training.
  • Cat Care: help with feeding, socializing, cuddling, and maintaining a clean, healthy, and safe environment for our cats.
  • Walks: assist our staff with walks to improve maintain physical fitness and improve socialization, confidence, and mood
  • Cleaning: assist our staff with morning, mid-day, and evening procedures.
  • Outreach: distribute brochures to your community (pet shops, coffee shops, groomers, vets, etc.).
